About Us

Our History & Values

Dexter & Associates, Inc. was found in 1997 by Rick Dexter. Rick acquired Rood & Associates that year after working as an outside salesperson for them since 1980. Douglas Brown joined Dexter & Associates in 1997 having worked as an outside salesperson for Rood & Associates since 1983. Rick & Doug service distributor accounts in a 5 state area including Ohio, Michigan, Kentucky, Western Pennsylvania and West Virginia.

The Principles we represent are a combination of domestic (USA) manufacturers, domestically situated manufacturers and direct import suppliers. All of the Principles are in a position to provide heat & chemical certifications, PPAP documents, full traceability documentation.
